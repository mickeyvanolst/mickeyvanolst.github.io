true / false

An art piece which poses the question between purpose and process. Constructing patterns and images though a laborious and methodic process, it portrays perpetual motion. Choreographed and intelligently steered through its algorithm, it carefully places each individual element in its right place. 

The piece has to be seen in motion in order to understand its mechanics. Behind a minimalistic appearance it encases a very elaborate and complex collection of circuitry and logics.
