Genesis

Combining a hotel, offices, gardens and a museum, Genesis forms an architectural space which offers a new kind of urban experience in China.

Together with Wolf Olins we developed a visual tool which explores the Daoist philosophical notion of ‘wu wei’, advocating instinctive living, being in the moment and letting change flow. Through this exploration five visual states were developed, each unique and constantly changing. Generating unique works of art which formulate a living and connected brand.
